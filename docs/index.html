RedCap

Screenshot Tool for macOS

RedCap is a lightweight screenshot utility that lets you capture any region of your screen — across multiple displays — with a single hotkey.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take a screenshot?
Press the global hotkey ⌘⇧1 to activate the capture overlay. Click and drag to select the region you want to capture, then release to save the screenshot.
What is the default hotkey?
The default shortcut is ⌘⇧1. The hotkey is active system-wide, even when RedCap is running in the background.
Where are screenshots saved?
Screenshots are saved to your Desktop by default. The file is named with a timestamp, e.g. screenshot-2026-06-16.png.
Does RedCap work across multiple displays?
Yes. The capture overlay spans all connected displays, so you can select a region across any screen in your setup.
How do I cancel a capture?
Press Escape at any time while the overlay is active to dismiss it without taking a screenshot.
Does RedCap run at login?
You can enable or disable "Launch at Login" from the menu bar icon. When enabled, RedCap starts automatically and stays ready in the background.
Is any data collected or uploaded?
No. RedCap processes everything locally on your device. No screenshots, metadata, or usage data is ever sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for details.
