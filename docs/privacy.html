Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

RedCap does not collect, store, or transmit any user data — ever. All screenshot activity stays entirely on your device.

No Data Collection

RedCap does not collect any personal information, usage statistics, or telemetry of any kind. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no connection to any external server.

Screenshots Are Processed Locally

All screenshots are captured and processed entirely on your Mac. Image data is written directly to your local disk and is never transmitted over the network. RedCap has no ability to access, read, or upload your screenshots.

No Analytics or Tracking

RedCap does not use any analytics services, crash reporters, or third-party SDKs. There is no tracking code of any kind embedded in the app. The following are explicitly absent:

  • Analytics (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
  • Crash reporting (e.g. Sentry, Crashlytics)
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Third-party data brokers or aggregators

No Network Requests

RedCap makes no outbound network requests during normal operation. The app does not phone home, check for updates via a remote server, or communicate with any external service.

macOS Permissions

RedCap requests only the permissions necessary to function as a screenshot tool:

  • Screen Recording — required to capture the contents of your screen
  • Accessibility — required to register a global hotkey and respond to it system-wide

These permissions are used solely within the app. No data captured through these permissions leaves your device.
